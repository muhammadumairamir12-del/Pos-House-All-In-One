About Us

Committed to Your Health & Wellbeing

Khan Clinic stands as a beacon of reliable, affordable, and quality healthcare services for individuals and families in Multan. Our foundation is built on a commitment to patient-centric care, integrating advanced medical practices with a compassionate approach. We understand that health is a journey, and we are here to support you every step of the way, focusing on both treatment and prevention.

Our clinic prides itself on a team of highly experienced and dedicated doctors, equipped with modern medical technology to ensure accurate diagnoses and effective treatments for all age groups. From general check-ups to specialized consultations, we maintain a patient-friendly environment where comfort and trust are paramount. Your health is our priority, and we strive to empower our community with accessible and superior healthcare solutions, fostering a healthier tomorrow.

Laboratory Services

Available Lab Tests

  • Blood Sugar Test (Random & Fasting) - For diabetes screening and management.
  • Complete Blood Count (CBC) with ESR - For detecting anemia, infection, and inflammation.
  • Cholesterol & Lipid Profile Test - For assessing cardiovascular disease risk.
  • Urine Examination (Complete) - For kidney function, urinary tract infections, and other metabolic conditions.
  • Liver Function Tests (LFTs) - To evaluate liver health and detect liver damage.
  • Kidney Function Tests (KFTs) - To assess kidney performance and detect renal issues.
  • Thyroid Function Tests (TFTs) - For diagnosing thyroid disorders like hypothyroidism or hyperthyroidism.
  • Hepatitis B & C Screening - For detecting viral hepatitis infections.
  • Blood Grouping & Rh Factor - Essential for transfusions and pregnancy.
  • Dengue PCR & Antigen Test - For early and accurate detection of Dengue fever.

Partner laboratory services available for accurate and timely testing, ensuring comprehensive diagnostic support and reliable results.
