FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide installation? +
Yes! Free installation is included with all software purchases. For hardware, our technician will set up everything at your location.
How long does installation take? +
Basic setup takes 2-4 hours. Complete setup with training takes 1 full day. We ensure everything is working perfectly before we leave.
Is the software FBR compliant? +
Yes, our Business and Enterprise plans are fully FBR (Federal Board of Revenue) compliant with IRIS integration for tax filing.
What if my hardware breaks? +
All hardware comes with manufacturer warranty. We provide repair and replacement services. Most repairs done within 24-48 hours.
Can I try before I buy? +
Yes! We offer a free demo at your location. See the software in action with your actual products before making any decision.
Do you deliver outside Lahore? +
We deliver hardware all across Pakistan via courier. For installation, we cover Lahore, Islamabad, Karachi, Faisalabad and major cities.
What payment methods do you accept? +
Cash, bank transfer, JazzCash, EasyPaisa and cheque. Easy installment plans available for large purchases.
Can I upgrade my plan later? +
Absolutely! You can upgrade from Starter to Business or Enterprise anytime. We will only charge the difference in price.
